About 2EH Consulting

2EH Consulting is a boutique consultancy that offers expert strategic planning services for local governments. With years of experience, we specialize in a range of services designed to help councils tackle urban growth challenges, infrastructure planning, and development.

Our team brings a blend of technical expertise and hands-on project management to ensure our clients receive the best solutions. We aim to create sustainable communities through the development of smart, data-driven infrastructure systems.
